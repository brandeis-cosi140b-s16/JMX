Hull City sign Michael Dawson from Tottenham for undisclosed fee


Hull City have completed the signing of Michael Dawson from Tottenham for an undisclosed fee.
 
The centre-half moves to the KC Stadium on a three-year contract after the clubs confirmed the deal was completed via their Twitter accounts.
 
“Michael Dawson has completed a move from Tottenham Hotspur for an undisclosed fee, the club are delighted to announce,” read a statement on City’s official website.
 
Tottenham are expected to pursue the Sevilla defender Federico Fazio to replace Dawson. The 27-year-old Argentinian has an £8m release clause.
