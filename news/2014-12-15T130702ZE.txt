b'Europa League draw: Liverpool face Besiktas in last 32'

The Florence-based side have been inconsistent in Serie A this season, but impressed in the earlier round of the Europa League, comfortably winning their group with one match to spare.</p> <p>Everton, who finished top of their Europa League group ahead of Wolfsburg, were the only British team to be seeded for the draw, and appear to have been handed the easiest tie \xe2\x80\x93 against Swiss side Young Boys \xe2\x80\x93 a team they have never competitively faced.</p> <p>Celtic narrowly progressed from Group D despite finishing with a negative goal difference and face Italian giants Internazionale, who were unbeaten in their group and re-appointed Roberto Mancini as head coach in November.</p> <p>Liverpool, Olympiakos, Zenit St Petersburg, Anderlecht, Roma, Ajax, Sporting Lisbon and Athletic Bilbao all enter the competition at this point, having finished in third place in their respective groups in this season\xe2\x80\x99s Champions League.</p> <p>The first-leg tie will be played on 19 February and the return leg is set for 26 February.</p> The Europa League round-of-32 draw in full <p>Young Boys v Everton</p> <p>Torino v Athletic Bilbao</p> <p>Sevilla v Borussia Monchedgladbach</p> <p>Wolsfburg v Sporting Lisbon</p> <p>Ajax v Legia Warsaw</p> <p>Aalborg v Club Brugge</p> <p>Anderlecht v Dynamo Moscow</p> <p>Dnipro v Olympiakos</p> <p>Trabzonspor v Napoli</p> <p>Guingamp v Dynamo Kiev</p> <p>Villarreal v FC Salzburg</p> <p>Roma v Feyernoord</p> <p>PSV Eindhoven v Zenit St Petersburg</p> <p>Liverpool v Besiktas</p> <p>Tottenham v Fiorentina</p> <p>Celtic v Internazionale</p>'
