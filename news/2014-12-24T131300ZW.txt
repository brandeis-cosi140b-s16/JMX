b'West Ham\xe2\x80\x99s Alex Song left out by Cameroon for Africa Cup of Nations'

The Africa Cup of Nations takes place in Equatorial Guinea from 17 January to 8 February.</p> <p>West Ham are expected to lose the Senegal defenders Diafra Sakho and Cheikhou Kouyat\xc3\xa9 for the tournament, with that squad scheduled to be announced on Friday.</p> <p>Cameroon\xe2\x80\x99s coach, Volker Finke, has ignored Song since this year\xe2\x80\x99s World Cup in Brazil.
