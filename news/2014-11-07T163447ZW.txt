b'Winston Bogarde applies for managerial position at Motherwell'


b'<p>Winston Bogarde is among the applicants for the Motherwell manager vacancy. The Dutchman, who won the Champions League in 1994, played for Ajax, Milan, Barcelona, and Chelsea, where he was widely criticised for seeing out a lucrative four-year contract despite making only a handful of appearances.</p> <p>Stuart McCall resigned at Motherwell last week and Bogarde is one of over 50 applicants who have put themselves forward for the position, according to BBC Sport. The 44-year-old former defender has been out of the game since retiring in 2005.</p> <p>McCall\xe2\x80\x99s former assistant Kenny Black, who was to oversee Motherwell\xe2\x80\x99s game with the Scottish Premiership leaders Dundee United on Friday, is one of the favourites for the job at Fir Park.</p> <p>After a promising start to the season, Motherwell had suffered five defeats in a row going into the Dundee United match.</p>'
