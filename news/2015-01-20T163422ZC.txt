b'Chelsea midfielder Marko Marin joins Anderlecht on loan for rest of season'


b'<p>German midfielder Marko Marin has joined Belgian champions Anderlecht - his third loan spell since signing for Chelsea in 2012.</p> <p>Marin, who has a contract at Stamford Bridge until 2017, spent the first half of the season on loan at Fiorentina having been at Sevilla last season. However the 25-year-old made no Serie A appearances and will now move to the Belgian Pro League for the remainder of the season.</p> <p>The Germany international has made just six Premier League appearances for Chelsea since joining from Werder Bremen for \xc2\xa37m.</p> <p>\xe2\x80\x9cRSC Anderlecht goes for quality and not quantity,\xe2\x80\x9d said Anderlecht\xe2\x80\x99s general manager Herman Van Holsbeeck.</p> <p>\xe2\x80\x9cThe arrival of Marko Marin is a win-win situation for both parties. Here he can play at the highest level and help the purple and white achieve its ambitions.\xe2\x80\x9d</p>'
