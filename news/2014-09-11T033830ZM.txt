b'Melbourne Victory bring defender Daniel Georgievski to A-League'

Playing in Australia, a country that means so much to me, is certainly an exciting opportunity and I can\xe2\x80\x99t wait to start training, meet the Victory players and get the season under way.\xe2\x80\x9d</p>'
